# Smart Subscription Manager 🚀

A modern, sleek, and intelligent subscription tracking application built with Flutter. Take control of your recurring expenses with a beautiful interface and smart insights.

## ✨ Features

- **📊 Comprehensive Dashboard**: View all your subscriptions at a glance with beautiful Material 3 cards.
- **🌓 Persistent Dark Mode**: Toggle between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved across app restarts using `shared_preferences`.
- **🖼️ Smart Logo Integration**: Automatically fetches high-quality logos for your subscriptions using the Hunter.io API.
- **🔄 Connectivity-Aware Loading**: Retries logo fetching automatically when internet becomes available.
- **📈 Spending Analytics**: Track your monthly and yearly expenses with interactive charts.
- **🔔 Smart Notifications**: Get reminded of upcoming payments before they hit your bank account.
- **📱 Custom Branding**: Features a unique, modern app icon and a polished splash screen.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Flutter](https://flutter.dev)
- **State Management**: [Provider](https://pub.dev/packages/provider)
- **Local Storage**: [Shared Preferences](https://pub.dev/packages/shared_preferences)
- **Charts**: [FL Chart](https://pub.dev/packages/fl_chart)
- **Animations**: [Flutter Animate](https://pub.dev/packages/flutter_animate)
- **Connectivity**: [Connectivity Plus](https://pub.dev/packages/connectivity_plus)
